Inspector Login

How it works

1.Inspector logs in with Inspector ID and PIN.
2.QR code opens inspect.html?id=AHM/HSE/FEX/001.
3.Checklist is submitted with optional photo.
4.Data is stored in Google Sheets and photo in Google Drive.
5.Dashboard shows checked, pending, and failed equipment.
